Resto is a highly flexible free Html5 & CSS3 template for the food delivery business. It comes with a light background in the hero header area that makes your images and content stand out with a clean and minimal look. Resto is 100% responsive and looks great on every device, whether a small smartphone screen or a large desktop screen. All the features and functions of this HTML5 template give it a sophisticated appearance. This responsive website template is an excellent option for anyone looking to establish a food delivery business.
